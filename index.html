Kitty Vault Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Kitty Vault ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile app that lets you scan photos of cats to discover their rarity, track your own cats, compete in quizzes and player-vs-player games, and discover local cat-related events. This policy explains what information the app collects, why, and how it's used.

Information we collect

Account information. If you create an account, we collect your email address and a display name you choose. You can also use the app as a guest without creating an account; guest data is tied to a device-generated ID rather than an email address.

Photos you capture or upload. To identify a cat's breed and rarity, the app uses your device camera (or photo library) to capture an image, which is uploaded to our backend for analysis and stored so you can view it again in your Vault later.

Location. With your permission, the app uses your approximate or precise location to sort nearby cat-related events by distance. Location is only used while you're using this feature and is not collected in the background.

Gameplay and social data. Display names, XP/level progress, quiz and challenge results, friend requests, and player-vs-player match history are stored so the app's leaderboards, friends list, and matchmaking features work. If you add friends or play against other players, your display name and gameplay stats become visible to them.

Push notification tokens. If you allow notifications, we store a device token (via Firebase Cloud Messaging) so we can notify you about things like friend requests, match results, and nearby events.

Analytics, crash, and performance data. We use Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Firebase Performance Monitoring to understand how the app is used, diagnose crashes, and measure app performance. This includes device information, app usage events, and crash logs. Firebase is operated by Google; see Google's privacy policy for how Google handles this data.

Advertising. The app shows ads and offers optional rewarded ads (watch an ad to earn in-app XP) through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and ad interaction data to serve and measure ads. See Google's privacy policy and How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps for details.

How we use this information

  • To provide the app's core features: cat scanning/rarity results, your personal Vault, quizzes, challenges, PVP matches, leaderboards, and friends.
  • To show nearby events and sort them by distance.
  • To send notifications you've opted into.
  • To diagnose bugs and crashes and improve app performance.
  • To serve ads and reward you with in-app XP for voluntarily watching them.

We do not sell your personal information.

Data storage and third parties

  • Account data, photos, gameplay data, and friend/social data are stored in our backend database (Supabase).
  • Analytics, crash reporting, performance monitoring, and push notifications are provided by Firebase (Google).
  • Ads are served by Google AdMob.

Your choices

  • You can use the app as a guest without providing an email address.
  • Location permission is optional; the events feature simply won't sort by distance if you decline it.
  • Notification permission is optional; you can decline or later disable it in your device settings.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.

Children's privacy

Kitty Vault is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
